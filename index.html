What is ShipSolver?

ShipSolver is a centralized platform for warehouse, transportation, and resource management. We empower small businesses and logistics providers to consolidate and automate their workflows.

About Us

We are an early-stage startup that is building a centralized platform for warehouse, transportation, and resource management. Our founders consist of two software engineers who are veterans of the Waterloo co-op program and have previously worked at companies such as Facebook and Splunk, and a logistics expert with over 20+ years of industry experience.
